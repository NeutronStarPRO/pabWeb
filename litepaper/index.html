WHAT IS PartyBoard

PartyBoard is a Meta-Social App, an immersive gamified social network with a token reward system. Users who have NFTs (Role/Dress Code) are able to interact with friends to earn PAT, which can be used to level up and mint new NFTs. PAT also allows for dressing up and social gaming.

Users can choose to lease or sell their NFTs on the in-app Marketplace; users'PAT earnings are stored in the in-app Wallet, which has a built-in Swap function.

HOW PARTYBOARD PROFIT

We collect a small amount of tax from in-app activities such as NFTs trading, minting, etc. Also, we provide a variety of pay-to-play social options and character dress-up models. All assets in the PartyBoard app are owned by individual users, and most of the revenue in the app is generated by and for users.

CREATOR ECONOMY

A portion of PARTYBOARD's profit goes to reward content or creators that are good for the public.

EASY TO USE

Through gamification and our connect2earn system, PartyBoard can deliver a more natural, vivid and warm social experience for millions of people.

NO BARRIERS TO ENTRY

PartyBoard does not require users to own NFT assets to connect2earn. Instead, they will be able to rent them for free from other users to get started, thanks to our revenue sharing model. (Coming soon)

LEARN AFTER EARN

Thanks to our easy-to-access social app, Non-crypto natives will be able to build the habit of socializing with PartyBoard without any preavious experience in crypto. They can earn and use tokens in natural social interactions before learning how to use a decentralized wallet so to make their first Web3 experience as smooth as possible.
